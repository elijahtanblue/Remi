What I Adjusted

Keep the billing unit as workspace. That is the economically relevant unit for your current go-to-market, not seats.

Split "pre-filter" into visibility and AI-worthiness. Permissions and product scope are not the same thing as cheap heuristics.

Do not model digest context growing forever. If a 6-month-old thread keeps feeding larger and larger prompts, the architecture needs a bounded summary state.

Use the cheaper summary model by default. Current pricing makes gpt-5-mini the more realistic everyday summary path, with gpt-5 reserved for escalations.

AI cost is usually not the thing that breaks the model. The flat-fee enterprise account with a huge workspace is still the risk case.
